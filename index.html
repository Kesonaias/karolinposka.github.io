Indonesian Traditional Dance

An exotic dance for Europe and specially to Estonia

The only professional Indonesian dancer in Estonia

Lived one year in Indonesia where I studyied dance in Yogyakarta art univeristy (ISI) and also learned from local students. I am proficient in Yogyakarta, Bali, East-Java and Kalimantan traditional dance styles.

Fire Dance

3 years of experience as a fire artist

Been training in Kelfiruis Fire Dance Crew with Margarita Kristall as my teacher. I dance with fire pois, torches and other fire objects.
